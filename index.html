Student Discipline Guidelines

Consolidated discipline policy · Levels of offenses & sanctions framework

Minor Offenses
  1. Failure to present an excuse letter for absences.
  2. Repeated tardiness (three [3] times in two [2] weeks).
  3. Failure to return reply slips, violation reports, and other official correspondences.
  4. Non-participation in flag ceremony, prayers, meetings, and other official school activities.
  5. Wearing of inappropriate attire, improper or incomplete school uniform, including P.E. uniform.
  6. Possession of gambling paraphernalia.
  7. Non-observance of good personal grooming and hygiene according to the standards prescribed by the school.
  8. Wearing of multiple earrings and/or dangling earrings for female students, and wearing of earrings, caps, or bonnets for male students.
  9. Loitering in restricted areas during class hours.
  10. Strolling around school premises after being sent home due to illness.
  11. Using and/or failure to switch off cellular phones and other electronic gadgets or devices during class hours, Holy Mass, or any other related activities.
  12. Use of abusive or indecent language.
  13. Any offenses analogous to those mentioned above.
Note: not incurring a sanction in a given period does not preclude the Discipline Office from later imposing a penalty for excessive or deliberate tardiness. Tardiness may be excused at the school's discretion if due to unavoidable circumstances.
